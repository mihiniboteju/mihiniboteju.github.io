At a very early age, I was amazed by the world I saw through the computer screen and it led me to wonder about the workings behind computers. I started discovering more about software programs, hardware components by reading articles, blogs, and by watching video contents online. This habit grew my curiosity about computers to a passion and now I am slowly planning to turn it into a rewarding profession.

In my Advanced-Level Mathematics, I was introduced to new areas such as differentiation, functions, trigonometry, series and integration. I keenly welcomed all these new areas and went over practicing different questions to make the concepts clear in my mind. As I followed Information Communication Technology (ICT) as a subject in my Advanced-Level class I gained a good understanding of basic computer programming, operating systems, computer networks, database systems and web development.
